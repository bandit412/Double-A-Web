Available Materials

These are engravable materials.

  • Wood (preferably clear of excessive grain and/or knots)
  • Leather
  • Plastics
  • Paper
  • *Glass (including mirrors)
  • *Annodized Aluminum

*Requires extra surface preparation.

These are engraveable surfaces:

  • Rectangular flat surface (max 18"x24")
  • Cylindrical surface (limited dimensions)

Available Finishes

These are only available for wood-based materials (extra cost will be applied to final project price).

  • Unfinished (no extra cost)
  • Clear, matte or glossy (cost depends on the size of the project)
  • Food safe finish (cost depends on size of the project)

All finished, with the possible exception of a food safe finish, are water-based finished.

Delivery Options

These are the standard options, other optoions may be available.

  • Pickup at location: FREE
  • Canada Post (standard costs determined by size and weight of the project)
  • Delivery: $0.50/km (to and from your location)
