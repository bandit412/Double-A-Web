What I Do

A typical engraving project starts with the selection of the engravable material (some materials may require preparation prior to engraving) and getting its dimensions so that I can set up the laser for the material. The next step is the design process which can include text, the desired font(s), images or photos, which are added to the design software; any custom design will require customer's approval before engraving starts. Next, the engraving starts, which is monitored to ensure the process proceeds correctly and to make sure that no portion of the engraving process exceeds 30 minutes (the laser needs a 10-15 minute cool down for every 30 minutes of engraving time). Finally, any required finishing processes are applied.
