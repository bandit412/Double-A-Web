Pricing

Pricing varies by design cost, material cost, and engraving time.

Example: For a small plaque, with a standard design, on a small wood plaque, and which has an engraving time of 3-4 hours @$3.00/hour, the cost would be:

Item Cost
Material $10.00
Design $4.00
Engraving Cost - 3 hours $9.00
*Total $23.00

*You do not have to pay GST on any engraving project.

Delivery charges may be added to the price of the project.

Payments

Any project/item that has already been created, a full payment is required prior to pickup. Any custom orders require a deposit of 50% of the project price, and the balance paid upon delivery/completion.

The following payment methods are accepted:

  • Cash
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Interact
  • e-transfer
